About Me

Hey, I'm Harry, a Marketing Executive rooted in Derbyshire. My career may be in marketing, but my heart is still firmly rooted in creative expression and the ever-evolving media landscape.

When I'm not wrapped up in marketing projects, you'll frequently discover me delving into the realm of cinema. I enjoy analyzing the subtleties of films and appreciating the artistry in each frame. Yet, there's a peculiar satisfaction in enjoying an intentionally exaggerated, over-the-top film. There's a certain freedom in not being overly serious and embracing the absurdity of a delightfully "bad" movie.

I'm also heavily into my music, lately, I've been captivated by Laufey's soulful blend of jazz and indie pop, creating a sonic experience that resonates deeply. Beabadoobee's indie rock, infused with '90s nostalgia, has become a staple on my playlists and, of course, Arctic Monkeys, with their timeless indie rock appeal and Alex Turner's clever lyricism, will always hold a special place in my musical heart.

In a nutshell, I'm a Marketing Executive with a deep love for all things media and a dedication to my work. I'm excited about the journey ahead, both professionally and personally, and I can't wait to see where it leads..
